AI for Business and Finance in Africa:
Challenges and Innovations

Explore how AI transforms Africa’s business and financial sectors. Join industry and academic leaders as they address regional challenges with innovative, ethical AI solutions.

Workshop at Deep Learning Indaba 2025

Date: 21 August 2025 · Kigali, Rwanda

Abstract

This workshop explores the intersection of artificial intelligence with business and financial sectors across Africa, focusing on developing and implementing AI solutions that address unique regional challenges. Through talks and collaborative discussions with industry and academic leaders, participants will investigate cutting-edge AI technologies and culturally aware approaches adapted for African markets. The workshop aims to bridge the gap between theoretical advances and practical implementation while ensuring ethical and equitable AI deployment for diverse African communities.

Goals & Objectives

  • Research Advancement: Spark novel AI research addressing African business and financial challenges and adapt state-of-the-art solutions to local contexts.

  • Capacity Building: Equip participants with advanced AI techniques and foster collaboration between researchers and industry practitioners.

  • Knowledge Exchange: Facilitate the sharing of datasets, tools, and practical insights for assessing AI performance in African business settings.

  • Success Metrics: Initiate collaborative projects, enhance paper quality, diversify participation, and boost post-workshop engagement.

Call for Papers & Pitches

We invite submissions of original research and applied work addressing the deployment of AI in business and finance across Africa. Accepted submissions will be presented as contributed talks or posters. The best paper will receive an award sponsored by our partners.

Paper Submission Instructions:
Submissions should be an extended abstract with a maximum of 4 pages (excluding references).
We encourage use of our double-column LaTeX template, but single or double column submissions in any format are acceptable.
We accept original work, work currently under review, or previously published work. The workshop is non-archival, so submissions will not preclude future publication.

Submission Deadline: 20 July 2025
New Submission Deadline: 03 August 2025

Submit your paper via OpenReview: Submit Here

In addition to research submissions, we are excited to launch a Call for Pitches for innovative AI product ideas. Individuals or groups are invited to propose a practical AI solution or startup idea addressing business or financial challenges in Africa.

Selected participants will pitch their ideas during our live Pitch & Feedback Session, receiving direct feedback from real-world investors at Askya Investment Partners. This is a unique opportunity to showcase your vision and connect with potential backers.

Pitch Submission Deadline: 31 July 2025
